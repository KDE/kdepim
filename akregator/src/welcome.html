Welcome to aKregator

Use the tree on the left to manage your RSS feeds.

  • Right-click a feed group (e.g. All Feeds) and choose:
    • Add to add a new feed via Add Feed dialog
    • Add feed group to add feed group, that help you organize your feeds in a tree
    • Delete to remove existing feed or feed group
    • Modify to edit existing feed or feed group
    • Fetch to fetch this feed. When clicked on a feed group will fetch from all feeds in this group and subgroups.

Click "Fetch all" to refresh all added feeds
