1. Introduction

The kmail Team welcomes you to KMail. The comfortable mailer for the K Desktop Environment. We set our goal towards making a beautiful & intuitive mailer on the one hand side but on the other making it as powerfull as possible. Please enjoy using kmail. Bug reports & suggestions should go to one of the developers or to the kmail mailing list.

We hope you will enjoy this Mailer,
